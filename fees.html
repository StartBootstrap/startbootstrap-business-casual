Overview

Presently, my out of network clinical fees are $200 for the initial consultation session (CPT 90791) and $165 for subsequent individual or family therapy sessions (CPT 90834 and 90847, respectively). Psychological Testing is billed at $200 per hour, with typical evaluations requiring eight to twelve billed hours, including the report.

In Network Insurance Coverage

Currently I participate with the following insurance plans:

Johns Hopkins EHP

Occasionally, there are other plans that utilize these networks for mental health, so patients are always encouraged to check their mental health benefits. Payment of deductibles, co-pays or any co-insurance is required at the time of the visit.

Out of Network Coverage

I am glad to help those patients participating with a Preferred Provider (PPO) or Point of Service (POS) plan in which I am not a provider with any necessary preauthorization forms and statements to maximize their out of network benefits. Patients are encouraged to contact their insurance plans to find out what type of authorization is needed, and what treatment plans need to be completed. They are also encouraged to check on any deductible amounts, what percentage will be covered once the deductible is met, and what the “reasonable and customary” amounts are for sessions.

Payment

Although I encourage check or cash payment at the time of service, I also accept Master Card, Discover and Visa as a courtesy to my patients. HSA and Flex Spending Cards may also be used.

If you believe you need immediate assistance, please call your local emergency number or proceed to the nearest emergency room.
